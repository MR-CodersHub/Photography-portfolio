Behind
The Lens

Every image tells a story. Here, we dive deeper into our most significant projects, exploring the creative process, technical challenges, and the raw emotion captured in every frame.

Nature Series

Golden Hour

Captured during the fleeting moments of twilight, this series explores the relationship between natural light and landscapes. We waited three days for the perfect conditions, ensuring every shadow and highlight told a story of serenity and warmth.

  • Location: Pacific Coast Highway
  • Gear: Sony A7R IV, 35mm f/1.4
  • Mood: Ethereal, Warm, Timeless
Portrait Series

Urban Soul

A study of human connection in the modern metropolis. This project strips away the noise of the city to focus on the individual. The high-contrast black and white processing emphasizes the raw emotion and character of each subject, creating a powerful narrative of resilience.

  • Location: Downtown Brooklyn
  • Gear: Leica Q2 Monochrom
  • Mood: Gritty, Authentic, Raw
Fashion Editorial

Vogue Vibes

Blending high fashion with street style, this editorial pushes the boundaries of conventional posing. We utilized dynamic angles and bold styling to create images that are as energetic as they are elegant. A true testament to the power of collaboration between artist and model.

  • Location: SoHo, NYC
  • Gear: Canon R5, 85mm f/1.2
  • Mood: Chic, Dynamic, Bold
