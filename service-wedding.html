Frequently Asked Questions

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes! We love destination weddings. Travel fees are calculated based on location and accommodation requirements.

How many photos will we receive?

For a standard 8-hour wedding, you can expect between 500-700 fully edited, high-resolution images.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a 30% non-refundable retainer and a signed contract to secure your date.
